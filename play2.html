PHIPPS.

Yes, my lord.

LORD GORING.

[_Holds up letter in pink envelope_.]

Ahem! Phipps, when did this letter arrive?

PHIPPS.

It was brought by hand just after your lordship went to the club.

LORD GORING.

That will do. [_Exit_ PHIPPS.] Lady Chiltern’s handwriting on Lady Chiltern’s pink notepaper. That is rather curious. I thought Robert was to write. Wonder what Lady Chiltern has got to say to me?
